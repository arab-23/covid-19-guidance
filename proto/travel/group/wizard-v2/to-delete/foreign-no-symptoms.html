Who can come to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms

This tool is up to date with the Orders in Council published on 2020-06-29

You indicated that:

  • you are a foreign national
  • you are not showing symptoms consistent with COVID-19

Are you one of these qualifying immediate family members of a Canadian citizen or of a landed permanent resident?

  • Spouse or common law partner
  • Dependent child
  • Dependent child of a dependent child
  • Parent
  • Grand-parent
  • Guardian or tutor of a Canadian child
  • None of the above immediate family member
DTO understanding of policy is if you are qualified immediate family you can come in, regardless of reason/working status, so this Q is before the big list.
Also, what if this person is say, a quarantine exempt doctor, who has a Canadian spouse? Answering "spouse" is technically correct, they can come in, but they will miss the details specific for the essential service provider and their potential quarantine exemption.
