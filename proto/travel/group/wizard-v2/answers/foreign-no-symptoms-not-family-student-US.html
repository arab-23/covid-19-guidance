Who can come to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms - Student - US

This tool is up to date with the Orders in Council published on 2020-06-29

You indicated that:

  • you are a foreign national
  • you are not showing symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • you are not an immediate family member of a Canadian citizen or a landed permanent resident
  • you are an international student
  • you are coming directly from the United States

Based on your answers, you may be allowed in Canada. The final decision is taken at the border, by a border officer.

  • Generally, you must follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days.
  • You may be exempt from quarantine if you are working in Canada as a student in a health field, as long as you do not directly care for persons 65 years of age or older within the 14-day period that begins on the day you enter Canada; a border officer will determine if you are exempt from quarantine
  • You may have to follow certain provincial or territorial requirements, depending on your destination.
