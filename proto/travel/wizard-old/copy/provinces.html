Provincial and territorial restrictions

There are currently travel restrictions and exemptions when moving between some provinces and territories. Exemption reasons (such as for essential workers, family members, property owners, transiting passengers etc.) vary by destination.

Always verify restrictions, requirements and exemptions with the province or territory before travelling within or to Canada.

Northern travel

Travel is being limited to prevent the spread of COVID-19. You may require documents, and may be required to quarantine at a designated location.

Find current travel guidance for the North:

  • Northwest Territories
  • Nunavut
  • Yukon

Atlantic province "travel bubble"

Participating provinces have agreed to form a “travel bubble” together. This means they are permitting travel between a specific group of provinces. They are restricting entry to people from provinces and territories not part of the “bubble.” You may require documents to declare your arrival.
