Who can come to Canada - Foreign national without symptoms - Crew

You indicated that:

  • you are a foreign national
  • you are not showing symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • you are an immediate family member of a Canadian citizen or a landed permanent resident
  • you are travelling for an essential reason
  • you are coming directly from the United States

Based on your answers, you may be allowed in Canada. The final decision is taken at the border, by a border officer.

Generally, you must follow a mandatory quarantine period of 14 days.

If you're travelling for an essential reason, you may be exempted from quarantine. For more information, go to What is essential work.
