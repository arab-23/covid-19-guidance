Who can come to Canada - Citizen with symptoms coming by land or sea

You indicated that:

  • you were a Canadian citizen, a permanent resident, a person registered under the Indian Act or a protected person
  • you are showing symptoms consistent with COVID-19
  • you are planning on coming by land or by sea

You can enter Canada by land or by sea. You will not be allowed by air.

  • If you have symptoms, you must follow a mandatory isolation period of 14 days.
  • You may have to follow certain provincial or territorial requirements, depending on your destination.
